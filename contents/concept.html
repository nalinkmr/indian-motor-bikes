FEW WORDS ABOUT OUR CONCEPT

WHO WE ARE?

Love travelling on bikes? Yes! Here is your chance to explore the unexplored places wherever you want. Wait for none and pack your bags to be a part of this voyage with India Motorbike tours. We will provide you the most adventurous life time experience on toughest routes in the world which are personally covered by us by designing special tour program for toughest riders who want to explore places across India. We work in unison with our parent company, BrahmaND TOUR and customize every kind of tour package as per the requirements & budget of our clients. Our team is well qualified and professional with minimum experience of 12 yrs. with multinational travel companies. With our bike tour packages we cover Great Himalayan Tours of Leh – Ladakh and Spiti valley (with a sudden rise and drop of altitude), Green Mountains of Kerala(covering some famous points of spice plantation) and Deserts of Rajasthan (Exploring the Indian Rajwadi culture) etc. So all you hard core riders or adventure lovers out there, come and be a part of our expedition to discover nature at its best. Our prime motive is to provide best bike tour packages which live up to the expectations of our clients.
